After nearly a decade, The Mavens Telescope is back with Endurance, an album exploring deep philosophical themes through progressive and alternative rock.
Formed in 2013 in Brazil by Thales Posella, Beto Ferrari and Jonas Ariel, The Mavens Telescope made their first appearance
on the progressive and alternative rock independent scene with their 2015 debut, The Seed of True Existence, featuring Marcio Herzer on bass.

Following a hiatus, Thales and Beto revived the project in 2020, reuniting with Herzer to craft Endurance, which was released on March 25th, 2025.
