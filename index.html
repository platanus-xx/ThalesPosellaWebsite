Silver Angel by Ivan Busic


On this project I've recorded all rhythm guitars using 2 small diaprahm AKGs as mic setup on each side.
I've recorded Piano using 2 AKGs as L+R.
Drums and vocals by Ivan Busic, bass by Andria Busic and additional centered guitar by Marco Bavini.
Backup vocals by Thales Posella and Marco Bavini.
I've mixed and mastered the track and also appeared as the piano player on the music video.




Nothing Else Matters by Metallica (cover by Thales Posella & Eduardo Deberaldini)


This was a sad but beautiful project I've recorded as a tribute to the passing of a very close friend, Eduardo Deberaldini.
I've recorded all acoustic, virtual instruments and vocals.
Old footage and sound from Eduardo Deberaldini on electric guitars were used postmortem.
I've mixed and mastered the track and edited the video.




Klimbim by Don Ross (cover by Thales Posella)


This is a cover from the beautiful song by Don Ross.
I had a great time recording my take on it.
The Mic setup is straight forward and can be seen on the video itself.




M.A.T. by Thales Posella


This is my entry for the Mateus Asato/Suhr contest. Although I didn't win, I got an honorable mention by Mateus and Suhr.
This features a custom made Strat straight into the Mesa Boogie Mark IV into a Laney 4x12-v30s cabnet.
Mic setup was 1 sm57 On-enter, another SM57 Off-center and a Ribon Mic handmade by @lisciel.




Sisters by Steve Vai (cover by Thales Posella)


This is a cover from the amazing Steve Vai.
This features a JEM 77FP 1989 straight to the Mesa Boogie MarkV into a Laney 4x12-v30s cabnet.
Mic setup was 2 sm57s, one On-enter and another Off-center. With an additional large diaphram AKG as room mic.




Wicked Game by Chris Isaack (piano version by Thales Posella)


This is a piano version I've made as a tribute to this beautiful song by Chris Isaack.
This features an original Steinwein 1929 upright piano.
Mic setup was 2 small diaphram AKGs on the piano and one large diaphram AKG on vocals.




The Mavens Telescope

I've produced, recorded all guitars, piano, synths, virtual instruments, backing vocals and mixed the whole album. It was mastered by Andy VanDette in NYC.
